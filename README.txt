Ostrom for Homey

This app connects your Ostrom energy contract to Homey, giving you real-time access to dynamic electricity prices and your energy consumption data.

Features:
- View current, lowest, and highest electricity prices of the day
- Track your total energy consumption from your Ostrom smart meter
- Create automations based on price conditions (e.g., start appliances when prices are low)
- Trigger flows when prices are below/above average, at daily lows/highs, or within custom price thresholds
- Optimize your energy usage by scheduling high-consumption tasks during the cheapest hours

Requirements:
- An active Ostrom energy contract in Germany
- Ostrom account credentials for pairing

Supported Flow Cards:
- Triggers: Price changes, price below/above average, price at lowest/highest
- Conditions: Current price comparisons, price ranking within time windows
